# Dad Jokes

*Certified groaners, aged in the garage and served with a thumbs-up.*

1. I told the thermostat a joke, but it didn't get it. The delivery was too dry — 68 degrees and holding.
2. Why did I bring a ladder to the family reunion? Because I heard the gathering was next level.
3. My lawn asked me for a bedtime story, so I read it a mow-moir.
4. I named our grill "Frank" so I can say I'm grilling with an old flame named Frank.
5. Why don't I trust the stairs in our new house? They're always up to something, and occasionally down to something.
6. I bought a wheelbarrow with a squeak so the yard work comes with its own soundtrack.
7. My kids asked why I talk to the dishwasher. I said we're just having a rinse-and-repeat conversation.
8. What do I call my car's turn signal? My blinker of few words.
9. I asked the hardware store guy for a stud finder, and he pointed at me. Great store. Ten out of ten.
10. Why did the spatula get promoted at my BBQ? It really knows how to flip the script.
11. The recliner and I have an understanding: I lean on it, and it never leans on me.
12. I told my son the WiFi password is "underthecouchcushions" because that's where everything else we lose ends up.
13. Why do I keep a pencil behind my ear at the lumber yard? In case I need to draw my own conclusions.
14. Our vacuum cleaner is my favorite band — it really picks things up around the house.
15. I don't always check the oil, but when I do, I dip-stick to the facts.
16. Why did I put a welcome mat in the garage? So the car feels appreciated after parallel parking.
17. My daughter asked if we were rich. I said, "Kiddo, we have a full punch card at the sandwich shop. We are one hoagie from glory."
18. What's my gardening philosophy? If at first you don't succeed, rake, rake again.
19. I whisper "good talk" to the smoke detector every time I take its battery out for the remote.
20. Why do I love the cul-de-sac? Because every drive ends on a positive turn.
21. The dryer keeps eating socks, so I started feeding it the ones with holes. Portion control.
22. I asked my reflection in the toaster for advice. It gave me a half-baked answer.
23. Why did the burger blush at my cookout? It saw the buns warming up to each other.
24. My tape measure and I are close. We've been through a lot of ups and downs, mostly 16 inches at a time.
25. I told the kids the minivan runs on complaints. Weirdly, we've never run out of fuel.
26. What did I say when the paint dried? "Finally, some closure on this whole shade-y situation."
27. I don't snore. I dream I'm a lawnmower, and I commit to the role.
28. Why did I bring a broom on the road trip? To sweep the family off their feet at every rest stop.
29. The garden hose asked for a raise. I said, "You're already the highest-pressure employee here."
30. My favorite exercise is the thermostat lunge — three steps, adjust, and retreat before anyone notices.
31. Why do I salute the mailbox? It's the only thing on this street that outranks me in deliveries.

37. The lawn gnome and I nod at each other every morning. Small yard, strong management team.
38. Why do I bring a cooler everywhere? Because someone in this family has to stay chill.

40. What did I tell the pancake that wouldn't flip? "Son, sometimes you gotta rise to the occasional."
